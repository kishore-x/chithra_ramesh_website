AT SRI MAHALAKSHMI MATRIC HR. SEC SCHOOL, WE CREATE A CLIMATE WHICH IS CONDUCIVE TO LEARNING

The highest academic standards with a challenging environment

Inaugurated in 1995, at Sri Mahalakshmi Matriculation Higher Secondary School, Sankarankovil, we are committed to creating a climate which is conducive to learning and ensures overall growth of the students.

We emphasize positive values such as respect, honesty, hard work, self-discipline, fairness and caring. Our school is considered among the best educational institutions in Sankarankovil, Tenkasi District. We strive to offer a holistic education that prepares students for board examination success and beyond. We are proud to consistently secure top marks and centums in the State Matriculation Board examinations.

Our reputation extends as one of the finest institutions in Tenkasi district, where we continue to set new standards in secondary and higher secondary education through regular workshops, technical partnerships, and extra-curricular training programs.
